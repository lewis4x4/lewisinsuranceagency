## Why Boat Insurance Matters

Your homeowners policy may provide limited coverage for small boats, but it typically has low limits and doesn't cover many common situations. A dedicated boat insurance policy provides comprehensive protection:

- **Hull coverage**: Physical damage to your boat from accidents, storms, or other covered events
- **Liability protection**: Covers damage you cause to other boats, docks, or injuries to others
- **Medical payments**: Covers medical expenses for you and your passengers
- **Uninsured boater coverage**: Protects you if another boater without insurance injures you or damages your boat
- **Personal property**: Fishing equipment, electronics, and gear on board
- **Trailer coverage**: Protects your boat trailer during transport and storage

## Suwannee River Considerations

The Suwannee River system presents specific considerations for boat owners. Tannin-stained water reduces visibility. Water levels change seasonally and after rain events. Submerged logs and debris require vigilance. Remote stretches mean help may be far away if something goes wrong.

Your insurance should account for where you actually boat, not just the theoretical value of your vessel.
