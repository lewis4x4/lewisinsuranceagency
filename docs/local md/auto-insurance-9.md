# Auto Insurance in Jacksonville, Florida

Jacksonville is Florida's largest city by area and one of its most populous. Driving here means navigating I-95, I-10, I-295, the Hart Bridge, and countless miles of urban and suburban roads. Whether you're commuting from the Westside to downtown, driving to the beaches, or running a delivery route, you need auto insurance that protects you in this high-traffic environment.

## What Jacksonville Drivers Face

Jacksonville's size and traffic create real risk. Major interstate interchanges, bridge traffic, beach access routes, and the daily commute all contribute to accident frequency. Florida's no-fault insurance system means even if someone else causes an accident, you may be limited in what you can recover without adequate coverage on your own policy.

Florida's minimum requirements—$10,000 PIP and $10,000 property damage—provide almost no protection in a city like Jacksonville. A single trip to Baptist or UF Health Jacksonville can exhaust your PIP benefits. A collision with any newer vehicle can exceed your property damage limits. When your coverage runs out, creditors pursue your wages and assets.

## Coverage That Actually Protects You

Lewis Insurance Agency works with multiple carriers to find coverage that fits your situation:

- **Liability coverage** that protects your assets—not just the legal minimum
- **Collision coverage** for damage to your vehicle in an accident
- **Comprehensive coverage** for theft, vandalism, and weather damage
- **Uninsured/underinsured motorist coverage** for accidents with drivers who have inadequate insurance (common in Florida)
- **Medical payments coverage** beyond basic PIP
- **Rental reimbursement** while your vehicle is being repaired
- **Roadside assistance** for breakdowns on Jacksonville's sprawling road system

## Multi-Vehicle and Bundle Discounts

Jacksonville households often have multiple vehicles. Insuring them together with one carrier typically saves money. Adding your homeowners, condo, or renters insurance can provide additional discounts.

Clean driving records, safety features, and completing defensive driving courses can also reduce your premiums. We'll explore every discount you qualify for.

- **Downtown commuters**: High-traffic area considerations
