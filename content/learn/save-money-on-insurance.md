## The Simple Answer

Shop your policies regularly, bundle where it makes sense, increase deductibles you can afford, and make sure you're getting every discount you qualify for. The goal isn't the cheapest policy—it's the best value for real protection.

---

## Features & Benefits

| Money-Saving Strategy | Potential Savings |
|----------------------|-------------------|
| Bundle home + auto with same carrier | 10%–25% |
| Increase deductibles | 10%–25% |
| Wind mitigation inspection (homeowners) | Up to 45% |
| Defensive driving course (auto) | 5%–15% |
| Pay annually instead of monthly | 5%–10% |
| Maintain good credit | Significant (varies by carrier) |
| Install security/safety devices | 5%–15% |
| Shop your policy every 2–3 years | Varies widely |
| Ask about loyalty discounts | 5%–10% |
| Remove unnecessary coverage | Varies |

**Smart Savings vs. Risky Cuts:**

✓ **Smart**: Higher deductible with emergency fund to cover it
✗ **Risky**: Dropping liability coverage to save a few dollars

✓ **Smart**: Removing collision on a 15-year-old car worth $2,000
✗ **Risky**: Dropping uninsured motorist coverage

✓ **Smart**: Shopping for better rates on the same coverage
✗ **Risky**: Buying minimum limits just because they're cheap
