## The Simple Answer

A Business Owners Policy bundles general liability and commercial property insurance into one package—usually at a lower cost than buying them separately. It's designed for small to medium-sized businesses and covers the most common risks in one convenient policy.

---

## Features & Benefits

| What's Included in a BOP | What It Covers |
|-------------------------|----------------|
| General Liability | Customer injuries, property damage, advertising claims |
| Commercial Property | Your building, equipment, inventory, furniture |
| Business Interruption | Lost income if you can't operate after a covered loss |
| Equipment Breakdown | HVAC, computers, refrigeration, machinery failures |
| Data Breach (optional) | Costs from hacking or data theft |
| Professional Liability (optional) | Errors and omissions in your services |

**Who Should Get a BOP:**
- Retail stores and restaurants
- Professional offices (accountants, consultants, agencies)
- Contractors and service businesses
- Small manufacturers
- Home-based businesses with inventory or equipment

**Why a BOP Makes Sense:**
- Bundled discount saves 10%–15% vs. separate policies
- One policy, one premium, one renewal date
- Easy to customize with add-ons for your industry
- Simplified coverage reduces gaps and overlaps
